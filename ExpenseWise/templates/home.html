Services

"Expense Wise" is an expense tracking app for students to manage their finances. It allows users to track and categorize expenses, set budgets, and visualize spending through a user-friendly dashboard, helping students stay within their budget and make informed financial decisions.

Detailed Tracking

Monitor and record daily expenditures across various categories, providing a clear overview of financial habits and trends.

Friendly UI

The app maintains simple but efficient workflow for the users to be able to work productively. Making it simple to understand even for a beginner.

Budget Management

Set personalized budgets for various categories and receive timely notifications to ensure financial goals are consistently met.

Real-Time Analytics

View real-time financial data through interactive charts and reports, offering valuable insights into spending patterns and financial health.

Supreme Student Council - Balayan Campus

SSC Balayan Campus is a student organization dedicated to fostering academic, social, and personal development for students at the Balayan Campus. As a central hub for student engagement, SSC Balayan provides opportunities for leadership, community involvement, and collaboration through various events, workshops, and initiatives. The organization aims to enhance the student experience by promoting a supportive and inclusive environment.
